City Destinations

New York City

New York City is a bustling metropolis known for its iconic landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, and Times Square.

Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Visitors flock to see landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, is known for its rich history, art, and architecture. Visitors can explore iconic landmarks such as the Colosseum, Vatican City, and the Trevi Fountain.

Shanghai

Shanghai, a global financial hub in China, is famous for its modern skyscrapers, historic waterfront, and vibrant culture. Visitors can experience attractions like the Bund, Yu Garden, and Nanjing Road.
